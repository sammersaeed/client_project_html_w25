Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

CAPS offers a wide variety of support options, such as Peer Support, Apps, and Coaching. The Support Options can be found on CAPS website. CAPS is committed to creating an environment based on our values of multicultural, multi-disciplinary and multi-theoretical practices that allow our diverse student body to access care, receive high quality services and take positive pathways to mental health.

Services Location

Services are provide virtually and in-person, on the 4th fourth floor of the Michigan Union. CAPS can also provide a private space for students to connect with their counselor via teletherapy.

Wellness Zone

There are is also a Wellness zone near the CAPS office, as well as within Pierpont and Munger. These zones are thanks to Wolverine Wellness, where students can relax and unwind using the following resources: massage chairs, light therapy, meditation tools, and gamess
