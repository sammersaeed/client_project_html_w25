Tutoring and Academic Resources

The University of Michigan School of Information has an Academic Success Team dediated to supporting students inside and outside of the classroom. They work to connect students with tutors and further acedemic resources. There are two types of Peer Tutoting available to students, Programming Peer Tutoring and Math Peer Tutoring. Tutoring covers specific classes in the UMSI program and UMSI MADS program.

Programming Tutoring

Tutoring is available for the following classes...

  • SI 106 Programs, Information, and People
  • SI 206 Data-Oriented Programming
  • SI 506 Programming I
  • SI 507 Intermediate Programming
  • SIADS 505 Data Manipulation (MADS)
  • General Python support (MADS)

Some tutors provide tutoring for other classes, which can be seen under their biographies. Please see the Programming Peer Tutoring Site for more information on how to sign up.

Math Tutoring

Tutoring is available for the following classes...

  • Linear Algebra (Available for MADS as well)
  • Statistics (Available for MADS as well)
  • General Math related course support (Available for MADS as well)
  • SIADS 502 Math Methods I (MADS)

Some tutors provide tutoring for other classes, which can be seen under their biographies. Please see the Math Peer Tutoring Site for more information on how to sign up.

Additional Resources

The Academic Success Team can also help connect students to reseources outside of UMSI, which include...

  • Writing Center
  • English Language Institute
  • LS&A Math Lab
  • Services for Students with Disabilities
